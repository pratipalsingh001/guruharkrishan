Welcome to GHKEI
​
We are very pleased that you are interested in learning more about GHKEI.  Admission is the first contact our school has with all the families who want to join us. It is through Admission that you will learn more about our community and we will have the chance to learn more about your family.

We are happy to open our classrooms for a tour and allow all new families to take a sneak peek of our students in action! Please contact us to schedule a school tour. After the tour, we will be happy to meet and talk about your hopes and expectations for your child’s educational setting.

Our goal is to assist you in making the best decision in this delicate, yet challenging moment, of choosing a school.
If you have any questions about the admissions process or about the programs offered, please do not hesitate to call or email us.
​
Once again, we’re thrilled you are considering GHKEI.
